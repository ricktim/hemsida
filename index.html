About Us

Evil Geniuses, often abbreviated as EG, is one of the oldest North American professional gaming organisations.

Founded in 1999, EG is known to have highly successful players in every competitive game they feature and is considered by many to be one of the most premier gaming organisations that exist today. EG was previously a member of the G7 Teams association.
